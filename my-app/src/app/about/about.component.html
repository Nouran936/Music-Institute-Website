Institute History

The institute has been established in 1988 and the number of the classrooms at this time was only 5 rooms in one branch specialized in teaching music and painting, now the institute became a 98 classrooms specialized in music and painting, and distributors on 9 branches and 11 hall, specified for ballet, gymnastics and taekwondo and distributed on 4 branches. Our institute was founded with the goal of nurturing musical talent and promoting music education. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most respected music institutions in the region.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality music education, fostering creativity and excellence in every student.

Vision

We aim to be a global leader in music education, empowering students with the knowledge and skills to make a positive impact in the music world.
